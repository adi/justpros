Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

What We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Your email address (for login and notifications)
  • Your name and profile information you provide
  • Posts and content you create

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve our service
  • To send you account-related emails (verification, password reset)
  • To display your public profile to other users

Third-Party Services

We use the following services:

  • Cloudflare - For security and performance (CDN, DDoS protection)
  • Resend - For sending transactional emails

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in our database hosted within the European Union. Your data never leaves the EU. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text.

Cookies

We use localStorage to store your authentication token. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data. From your Settings page, you can:

  • Edit your profile - Update your handle, name, headline, and skills anytime
  • Export your data - Download all your data in JSON format
  • Delete your account - Permanently remove your account and all associated data

No need to contact us - you can do it yourself, instantly.
